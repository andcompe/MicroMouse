<h1>Instructions MicroMouse</h1>

Some tips before you start
<ul>
<li>The plastic is thin where it should be folded. Feel free to check 2 times in which direction it should be folded so that it will be correct from the start. If you fold back and forth, it will come off.</li>
<li>The glue we use is cyanoacrylate, also called superglue or instant glue.
    <ul>
      <li>Cyanoacrylate is basically not dangerous, but can be difficult to remove. Here is a link to the Poison<a href="https://giftinformation.se/kemikalieregister/cyanoakrylatlim/"> Information Center,</a> read it before anything happens. 
      </li><li>It adheres incredibly well to the plastic, but also to fingers and a lot of other things. Just use A LITTLE glue, it just gets messy with too much. And you get it on your fingers and then your fingers get stuck in the plastic and you get ugly spots left. So just use a little glue.</li>
      <li>If you get glue on your fingers, wipe off with paper as soon as possible. What you can't remove will dry quickly and then it's easiest to have mechanical abrasion to remove it. Sandpaper also works well with the glue on the fingers.</li>
      <li>Cyanoacrylate can react with certain textiles, eg cotton, and can then develop heat that can lead to burns. Watch out for the clothes. </li>
    </ul>  
</li></ul>
